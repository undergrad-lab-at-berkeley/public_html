LaTeX Lecture I

The LaTeX series is a 2-part event hosted by librarian Sam Tepliztky (and colleagues) from the Engineering and Physical Sciences Libraries at Berkeley. Part 1 covers a basic overview of LaTeX for beginners as well as simple text and math commands.

Video: [28 min] https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zm1BG6CytaE



Follow along on github https://github.com/EPS-Libraries-Berkeley/LaTeX or via the supplementary documents:

LaTeX Lecture II
